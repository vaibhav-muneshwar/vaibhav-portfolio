🌱 JeevaTraksha: Embedded AI for Multi-Crop Disease Diagnosis

92.5% Accuracy • Real-Time

  • ✔ Multi-crop support (Paddy, Cotton, Tomato, etc.)
  • ✔ CNN-based classification (MobileNetV2)
  • ✔ Image processing: K-Means, GLCM, Thresholding
  • ✔ Deployable on Raspberry Pi (Edge AI)
