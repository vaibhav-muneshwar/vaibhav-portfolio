About My Approach

With a strong foundation in embedded systems and a passion for real-world reliability, I focus on designing firmware and edge solutions that perform under constraints — limited power, intermittent connectivity, and harsh environments.

Whether optimizing inference on low-cost edge hardware for agriculture or building secure, hardware-backed IoT layers, my goal is to turn complex simulations into dependable silicon that solves practical problems, especially in Indian contexts like smart farming and emergency response systems.

I believe great engineering lies in the details: clean interrupt handling, robust error recovery, and measurable improvements in latency, power, and uptime. Currently open to R&D collaborations that push the boundaries of edge intelligence and secure embedded systems.
